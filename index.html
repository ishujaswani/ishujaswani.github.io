About Me

My passion for finance, nurtured by my father's success as an equity investor, has fueled my drive to become a lifelong student in the field. My journey in finance began at an early age, as I closely followed financial news with my father, gradually developing a deep understanding of financial numbers. This early exposure motivated me to start investing in the stock market, which quickly became a passion. To expand my knowledge, I pursued an undergraduate degree in finance and accounting, focusing on understanding balance sheets. Additionally, I completed CFA Level 1 to gain a comprehensive knowledge of finance and the ability to make informed investment decisions. While studying for CFA level 1, I developed a strong liking for quantitative analysis. This got reassured when I received a 95 percentile in quantitative analysis in CFA level 1. Therefore, I went ahead with a postgraduate diploma in economics, econometrics, and data analytics. This program equipped me with the necessary skills to analyze and leverage data in the financial industry. Currently, I am pursuing a Masters's in Applied Analytics to further develop my ability to analyze complex financial data and forecast future trends.
